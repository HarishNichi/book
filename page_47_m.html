They entered the library. It was a vast cavern, filled with towering shelves of scrolls and books. The air was thick with the scent of old paper and dust. A faint light emanated from glowing crystals embedded in the ceiling, casting a soft, ethereal glow on the ancient texts.

"Where do we even begin?" Kaelen asked, his voice a low whisper.

Elias was already poring over a large, leather-bound tome. "We search for any mention of the Alchemist King, the Crimson Pendant, or the symbol Anirudh wears."

They spent days in the library, reading through countless scrolls and books. They learned more about the Alchemist King, a benevolent ruler and a master of a forgotten form of magic that blended science and sorcery. They learned about his enemies, a shadowy cabal who sought to steal his power for their own nefarious purposes.
