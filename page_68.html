The arena roared. Anirudh faced his next opponent, Vana, The Sound Bender. Vana stepped into the ring, an unnerving stillness surrounding her despite the cacophony of the crowd. She raised her hands, and the air itself seemed to hum.

"The sound," Arjun's voice echoed in his memory, "it is a distraction. Find the stillness within."
