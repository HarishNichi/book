The successful completion of the team trial earned both Anirudh and Rohan a place in the top ten. The announcement was met with surprise by many of the students, who had expected Rohan to succeed, but not the orphan boy he had so openly despised.

Rohan, for his part, accepted the result with a newfound maturity. He gave Anirudh a nod of acknowledgment, a silent gesture that spoke volumes. The rivalry was not over, but it had changed. It was no longer about a noble versus an orphan, but about two skilled warriors pushing each other to be better.
