He learned to heal with a touch, to command the elements, to walk in the world of spirits. He became a legend, a whisper on the wind, a story told by travelers and bards. He was the Ghost of the North, the Shadow of the East, the Dragon of the West.

But he never forgot his true purpose. He was the heir of the Alchemist King, the guardian of the Crimson Pendant, and he had a destiny to fulfill. He knew that one day, he would have to face the ultimate darkness, the source of all the evil in the world. And he would be ready.
