When he left the monastery, he was a changed man. The fire in his eyes was still there, but it was now tempered with a quiet wisdom. He was ready to face his destiny, to confront the darkness that had haunted him for so long.

His journey took him to the ancient city of a hundred spires, a place of great learning and terrible secrets. It was here, he had learned, that the cult had its stronghold. It was here that he would find the answers he sought, and the enemies he had to defeat.
