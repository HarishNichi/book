Meanwhile, Elara, Kaelen, and Elias continued their own quest. They traveled to the far corners of the world, following the clues left behind by the Alchemist King's wife. They faced countless dangers, from treacherous landscapes to monstrous beasts, but they never wavered in their mission.

They learned more about the prophecy, of a chosen one who would unite the scattered factions of the world and lead them against the coming darkness. They knew that Anirudh was this chosen one, and they had to find him before it was too late.
