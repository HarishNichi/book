They burst out of the prison and into the cool night air. They were in a courtyard, surrounded by high walls. The main gate was heavily guarded. There was no way out.

"We're trapped," Elias said, his voice filled with despair.

But Elara had an idea. She saw a series of wooden scaffolding along one of the walls, used for repairs. "There," she said, pointing. "If we can reach the top, we can get over the wall."

It was a risky climb, but it was their only chance. They would have to be quick and quiet. The guards were already searching the prison for them.
