Act 2: Rise of the Storm

Scene 8: The Master's Acknowledgment

The air in the hidden dojo was still, the last rays of sunset casting long shadows across the polished floor. Anirudh stood before Arjun, his posture different now – not just a student, but someone who had faced the storm and found his own center within it.

Arjun’s eyes, usually alight with intensity, were soft with a quiet understanding.

ARJUN (softly)

You have surpassed my teachings, Anirudh.
