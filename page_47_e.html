The moment came during the changing of the guards. As the new guard settled into his post, Kaelen put his plan into action. With a mighty heave, he slammed his shoulder against the weak point in the wall. The mortar cracked, and with a second, deafening blow, the stones gave way.

The guard, startled, rushed towards Kaelen's cell. At the same moment, Elara placed her hands on the bars of her own cell, her eyes closed in concentration. She poured her energy into the magical ward, a wave of disruptive force that caused the lock to spark and sputter.

The guard, distracted by Kaelen, was unprepared for the sudden attack from Elias. The scholar, using a sharpened piece of rock he had managed to break from his cell wall, stabbed at the guard's hand, causing him to drop the keys.
