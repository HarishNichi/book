After leaving the Kanjiram Academy, Anirudh began his journey into the wider world. He was no longer just a student; he was a warrior on a quest. He traveled from city to city, from village to village, always searching for clues about his past, for any mention of the Alchemist King or the Crimson Pendant.

He took on odd jobs to survive, working as a mercenary, a guard, a guide. He fought in underground tournaments, testing his skills against the best fighters in the land. He honed his Vayu Vajra form, his movements becoming more fluid, more powerful, more his own.
