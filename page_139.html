Act 2: Rise of the Storm

Scene 8: The Master's Acknowledgment

INT. HIDDEN DOJO - SUNSET

Arjun watched Anirudh. There was a stillness about the young man now, a quiet confidence that hadn't been there before. The journey through the Whispering Scrolls, the animal trials, the bruising defeat in the tournament, and the real-world peril of his village had forged something new within him.

Anirudh stood tall, the setting sun casting long shadows behind him. The air around him seemed to shimmer faintly, a subtle energy flowing just beneath his skin.
