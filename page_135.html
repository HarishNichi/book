Act 2: Rise of the Storm

Scene 8: The Master's Acknowledgment (Continued)

Arjun stepped back, a rare look of pride on his face.

ARJUN
(softly)
You have surpassed my teachings, Anirudh.

Anirudh lowered his head, a sense of peace washing over him. He hadn't sought to exceed his master, only to find his own truth.

ANIRUDH
Not surpassed. Only accepted who I am.

The air in the hidden dojo shifted. The dynamic between master and student dissolved, replaced by a quiet understanding.
