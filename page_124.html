Act 2: Rise of the Storm

Scene 7: Rebirth of Will

Anirudh stood before the incomplete scroll in the mountain cave. The flickering torchlight danced across the ancient parchment, revealing diagrams and text that had once seemed cryptic and frustratingly out of reach. But the recent battle, the raw fear he had faced protecting his home, had stripped away the layers of inherited ambition and forced him to confront his own core.

He remembered the fluid evasion learned from the Flame Serpent, the grounded power of the Ice Tiger, and the elusive movements of the Phantom Crow. They weren't just techniques to be copied; they were principles to be understood.
