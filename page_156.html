In the city of a hundred spires, Anirudh found a city living in fear. The cult's influence was everywhere. Their spies were in the markets, their enforcers on the streets. The people lived in a state of constant anxiety, their faces etched with worry.

Anirudh moved through the city like a ghost, gathering information, learning the cult's routines, their strengths, their weaknesses. He found a small group of rebels, a handful of brave souls who dared to defy the cult's rule. He joined them, his skills and his determination a welcome addition to their meager forces.
