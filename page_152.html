In the city of a thousand temples, he met a wise old woman who was said to be a seer. He showed her the pendant, and her eyes widened in recognition. "This is a key," she whispered, her voice a dry rustle of leaves. "A key to a forgotten power. It will lead you to your destiny, but it will also attract the darkness."

She warned him of the shadows that were hunting him, of a cult that sought to claim his power for their own. She told him to be wary, to trust no one, and to always follow the light within him.
