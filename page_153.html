Anirudh took her words to heart. He became more cautious, more aware of his surroundings. He learned to move like a shadow, to blend in with the crowd, to be invisible when he needed to be. He was no longer just a warrior; he was a hunter, and he was being hunted.

He followed the trail of the cult, the whispers of their dark deeds leading him across the continent. He learned of their rituals, their sacrifices, their relentless search for the heir of the Alchemist King. He knew that he was on a collision course with them, and that a final confrontation was inevitable.
