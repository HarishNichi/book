Act 2: Rise of the Storm

Scene 8: The Master's Acknowledgment (Pages 87-150)

Anirudh felt the weight of Arjun's words, not as a burden, but as an affirmation. He wasn't meant to simply replicate, but to integrate, to forge his own path from the lessons of his past.

Arjun bowed his head slightly, a gesture of profound respect that transcended their previous roles. "You have surpassed my teachings, Anirudh," he said, his voice a low murmur in the fading light.

Anirudh met his gaze, a quiet strength in his own eyes. "Not surpassed," he replied, a small smile playing on his lips. "Only accepted who I am."
