In a final, desperate move, Anirudh unleashed the full power of his Vayu Vajra form. A torrent of golden energy erupted from him, a miniature sun that engulfed the cult leader. The sorcerer screamed, his body dissolving into dust, his dark magic consumed by the light.

Anirudh collapsed, his energy spent. He had won. The cult was defeated, the city was free. But the victory came at a great cost. The rebellion had been all but wiped out, and Anirudh was once again alone.
