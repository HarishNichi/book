Act 2: Rise of the Storm

Scene: Village in Peril (Continued)

The air crackled with dark energy. Twisted creatures, their forms grotesque and menacing, emerged from the shadows, drawn by an unseen force. Anirudh's hometown, usually peaceful and vibrant, was now a scene of chaos and fear.

He saw the desperation in the eyes of his people, the raw terror as they fled from the corrupted beasts. It was a stark contrast to the controlled environment of the arena, a brutal reminder of the stakes in the real world.

Arjun's words echoed in his mind: "This is your test. Not in an arena. In the real world."

He felt the familiar surge of anger, the instinct to rush in with brute force, to unleash everything he had learned in a whirlwind of power. But something held him back.
