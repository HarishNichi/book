Act 2: Rise of the Storm

Scene 8: The Master's Acknowledgment

INT. HIDDEN DOJO - SUNSET

Arjun watched Anirudh. There was a stillness about the young man now, a quiet power that hadn't been there before. The raw edge of youthful ambition had been honed by hardship and introspection.

ARJUN

(softly)

You have surpassed my teachings, Anirudh.

Anirudh met Arjun's gaze, his expression calm and centered.

ANIRUDH

Not surpassed. Only accepted who I am.

A faint smile touched Arjun's lips. He understood. Anirudh was no longer trying to emulate his father or follow Arjun's path; he had found his own.
