They made it. Battered and bruised, but they were free. They disappeared into the darkness of the surrounding forest, the shouts of the guards fading behind them. They had escaped, but they knew their journey was far from over.

"We have to find the child," Elara said, her voice filled with a new sense of urgency. "We have to protect him."

Elias nodded. "I have a map," he said, pulling a worn piece of parchment from his tunic. "It's not much, but it's a start. It shows the location of a hidden library, a repository of ancient knowledge. It might have information about the Crimson Pendant."

And so, their new quest began. Three unlikely companions, bound by a shared purpose, on a journey to find a boy who held the fate of the world in his hands.
