They reached the top of the wall, the wind whipping at their clothes. Below them, the courtyard was swarming with guards. They had to jump. Kaelen went first, landing with a heavy thud on the other side. He looked up, his face grim.

"It's too high for you," he said to Elara and Elias. "You'll break a leg."

But Elara had another idea. She took off her cloak and, with Kaelen's help, fashioned a makeshift rope. It was a desperate gamble, but it was their only hope.
