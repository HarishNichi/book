Their journey took them to the bustling port city of Silverhaven. They needed to find a ship to cross the sea to the lands where the Alchemist King's child was hidden. The city was a maze of narrow streets, crowded markets, and shadowy alleys.

They soon realized that they were being followed. The cult had not given up their pursuit. They were being hunted, and they had to be careful. Every shadow could hide an enemy, every friendly face could be a mask.
