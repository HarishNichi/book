From the rift, a creature of pure darkness emerged. It was a being of shadow and malice, its form constantly shifting, its eyes burning with a cold, malevolent light. It was the entity the cult worshipped, the source of their dark power.

The creature was a formidable opponent, its power far greater than anything Anirudh had ever faced. It fed on fear, on doubt, on despair. It was a creature of the void, and it sought to consume the world.
