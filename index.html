The wind howled, tearing at the weak walls of the hut. Heavy rain beat down on the roof. Inside, it smelled of damp earth and fear. Water dripped through cracks in the walls, making the dirt floor muddy. A single oil lamp flickered, making long shadows.

WOMAN

(whispering, crying) Anirudh... my little warrior. Forgive me for bringing you into this world of shadows... forgive me that I can't protect you anymore.

Her shaking fingers reached for a copper chain around her neck. On it was a pendant. It was not fancy, but it felt heavy with a strange power. It was round, made of dark copper, with a swirling symbol carved into it. As she held it, the symbol glowed with a soft, golden light.

With great effort, she put the chain over the baby’s head. The cool metal touched his skin. The glowing symbol seemed to get brighter.

WOMAN (V.O.)

Your father’s blood is in you, little one. The blood of the Alchemist King. They hunt for it... they will hunt you. But you must live... you must survive... even if I can't watch you grow up.

The storm grew stronger. The lamp flickered, and the woman struggled to breathe. She was in great pain, but she held her baby tightly to protect him.
WOMAN (her voice weak) Anirudh... Don't let their darkness touch you... Carry the light... Find your strength...
Her cold, shaking fingers touched the pendant on the baby’s neck. Its glow seemed to pulse with her fading heartbeat. She wanted more time, but she was running out of it. Thunder crashed, and the hut's door was thrown open with a crash.
A tall, cloaked figure stood in the doorway. The woman stared in terror. The man stepped inside, and the world went dark for her.
