Their paths finally crossed in the ancient city of a hundred spires. Anirudh, now a seasoned warrior, was investigating a resurgence of the cult he had defeated years ago. Elara, Kaelen, and Elias had come to the city following a clue from the queen's journal.

They met in a crowded marketplace, a chance encounter that felt like the hand of destiny. Elara recognized the symbol on Anirudh's pendant, the same symbol from her visions and the queen's journal. She knew, with a certainty that shook her to the core, that she had found him.
