The crowd roared, a mixture of awe and dread. They had never seen such raw, devastating power. Anirudh felt his energy reserves dwindling, his body aching with each impact.

Anirudh brought up his arms, a desperate attempt to shield himself. The impact was immense, a shockwave that threw him clear across the arena. He hit the far wall with a sickening thud, his vision blurring, the sounds of the crowd fading.
