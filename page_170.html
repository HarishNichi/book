The rift closed, the darkness consumed by the light. The creature was gone, its threat vanquished. The temple was silent, the air still crackling with residual energy. Anirudh, Elara, Kaelen, and Elias stood together, battered and weary, but victorious.

They had won the battle, but they knew the war was not over. The darkness would return, in other forms, in other places. But they would be ready. They were a team, a family, and they would face whatever came next, together.

The story of the Child of the Crimson Pendant was not over. It was just beginning.
