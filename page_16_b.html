The final trial before the selection of the top ten was a team-based exercise. The students were divided into pairs and had to navigate a treacherous, magically altered forest to retrieve a hidden banner. To Anirudh's dismay, he was paired with Rohan.

Rohan made his displeasure clear. "Just stay out of my way, orphan," he sneered. "I'll get the banner myself. I don't need a liability."

Anirudh knew that they would have to work together to succeed. The forest was too dangerous for one person to handle alone. He would have to find a way to make Rohan see that, or they would both fail.
