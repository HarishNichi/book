Together, they faced the resurgent cult. This time, Anirudh was not alone. He had allies, friends, a family forged in the crucible of shared purpose. Elara's magic, Kaelen's strength, and Elias's wisdom, combined with Anirudh's own power, were a force to be reckoned with.

They fought their way to the heart of the cult's new stronghold, a dark fortress in the mountains. There, they discovered the source of the cult's power: a rift in the fabric of reality, a gateway to a realm of darkness.
