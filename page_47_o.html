Kaelen placed a hand on her shoulder, his touch a comforting weight. "We will find him," he said, his voice a low, steady rumble. "We will protect him."

Elias, his eyes shining with unshed tears, looked at the journal. "This is more than just a story," he said. "It is a testament. A legacy. We are now a part of it."

They left the library with a renewed sense of purpose. They were no longer just adventurers seeking knowledge. They were guardians, protectors of a lost heir, and they would not fail in their duty.
