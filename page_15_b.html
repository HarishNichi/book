Rohan's provocations escalated. During a sparring session, he "accidentally" used excessive force, leaving Anirudh with a sprained wrist. The instructor, a man who favored the noble-born students, dismissed it as a training accident.

Anirudh, however, knew it was a deliberate act. He spent the next few days training with his non-dominant hand, his determination burning brighter than ever. He would not give Rohan the satisfaction of seeing him falter. He adapted, he overcame, and in doing so, he grew stronger.
