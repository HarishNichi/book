In the cell next to Elara and Kaelen, a new prisoner arrived. He was a man with a wiry frame, a sharp, intelligent face, and spectacles perched on his nose. He introduced himself as Elias, a scholar from the Royal University. He had been captured weeks ago while studying the ancient ruins.

Elias was not a warrior, but he had a sharp mind and a vast knowledge of history and lore. He had been observing their captors, a cult of fanatics who worshipped a shadowy entity. He had overheard their plans, their whispers of a "Child of the Crimson Pendant" and a "rising storm."
