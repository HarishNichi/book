Anirudh reached the inner sanctum of the temple. There, he came face to face with the cult's leader, a man with eyes as black as the void and a voice that dripped with malice. He was a powerful sorcerer, his body crackling with dark energy.

"So, the heir has finally come to me," the cult leader said, a cruel smile on his lips. "I have been waiting for you. Your power will be mine."

Anirudh stood his ground, his hand on the hilt of his sword. He was not afraid. He had faced death before, and he was ready to face it again.
