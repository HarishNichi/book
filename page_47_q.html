In a crowded tavern, they met a grizzled sea captain named Jax. He was a man with a weathered face, a mischievous glint in his eyes, and a reputation for being the best sailor in Silverhaven. He was also a man who was not afraid of danger, for the right price.

They told him their story, not all of it, but enough to convince him of the urgency of their quest. Jax, a man who had seen his share of strange things in his long life, agreed to take them. He was intrigued by their story, and the promise of a hefty reward was a powerful motivator.
