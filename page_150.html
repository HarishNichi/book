The Master's Acknowledgment

INT. HIDDEN DOJO - SUNSET

ARJUN
(softly)
You have surpassed my teachings, Anirudh.

ANIRUDH
Not surpassed. Only accepted who I am.

Arjun bows. Not as a master to a student—but as equals.

A new invitation arrives. “War Council of Realms”—an elite gathering where his father was once betrayed.

Anirudh opens it. His path leads next to uncover the truth behind his birth—and face ancient enemies.
