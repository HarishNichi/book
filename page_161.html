He left the city of a hundred spires, a heavy weight on his heart. He had won the battle, but the war was far from over. He knew that the cult was just one manifestation of the darkness that threatened the world. There were other forces at play, other enemies lurking in the shadows.

His journey continued, his path leading him to the far corners of the world. He sought out ancient masters, learning new skills, new forms of magic. He grew stronger, wiser, more attuned to the power that flowed within him.
