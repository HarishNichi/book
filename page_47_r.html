They set sail on Jax's ship, the Sea Serpent. It was a sturdy vessel, built to withstand the harshest storms. The sea was calm at first, but soon, the sky darkened, and a fierce storm descended upon them. The waves were like mountains, crashing over the deck, threatening to tear the ship apart.

It was not a natural storm. Elara could feel the dark magic in the air, the same magic that had been present in the ruins. The cult was trying to stop them, to send them to a watery grave.
