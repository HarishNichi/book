Act 2: Rise of the Storm

Scene: Rebirth of Will

Anirudh walked deeper into the ancient cave, the air thick with the scent of damp earth and forgotten power. The incomplete scroll lay open in his hand, its cryptic symbols now seeming to shift and align with a newfound clarity in his mind.

The trials against the elemental beasts, the sting of defeat against Rudrak, and the raw fear he’d faced protecting his village – it all converged. He wasn't just learning techniques; he was learning about himself.
