Act 2: Rise of the Storm

Scene 8: The Master's Acknowledgment (Continued)

Anirudh stepped back, a quiet confidence in his stance. "Not surpassed," he corrected softly. "Only accepted who I am."

Arjun's eyes, ancient and wise, held Anirudh's gaze for a long moment. A slow smile spread across the master's face.

Then, in a gesture that spoke volumes, Arjun bowed. It was not the bow of a master to a student, but of equals acknowledging each other's journey.
