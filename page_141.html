Act 2: Rise of the Storm

Scene 8: The Master's Acknowledgment (Continued)

Arjun stepped back, a rare, genuine smile on his face. "You have surpassed my teachings, Anirudh."

Anirudh met his gaze, a quiet confidence replacing his usual intensity. "Not surpassed," he replied softly. "Only accepted who I am."

There was a moment of silence, a profound understanding passing between master and former student.
