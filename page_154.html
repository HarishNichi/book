In the frozen wastes of the north, he found a hidden monastery, a place of quiet contemplation and intense martial training. He was taken in by the monks, who saw the fire in his eyes and the weight of his destiny. They taught him a new form of meditation, a way to control the flow of his energy, to calm the storm within him.

He spent a year in the monastery, his body and mind growing stronger. He learned to control his power, to use it with precision and purpose. He was no longer a raw, untamed force, but a finely honed weapon, ready for the war that was to come.
