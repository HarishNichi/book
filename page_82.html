Act 2: Rise of the Storm

Scene 6: Village in Peril

Anirudh, grim-faced, nodded. He understood. This was not a theoretical exercise in a controlled environment. This was real. The safety of his home, the lives of the people he grew up with, depended on his newfound skills and judgment.

"I understand," Anirudh said, his voice firm. "I will go immediately."

Arjun placed a hand on his shoulder, a rare gesture of warmth.

ARJUN

This is your test. Not in an arena. In the real world.

Anirudh looked at the distant mountains where his village lay, shrouded in a growing sense of dread. He turned and began to descend, leaving the quiet solitude of the temple for the urgent chaos that awaited him.
