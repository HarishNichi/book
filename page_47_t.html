As dawn broke, the storm subsided. The sky cleared, and the sea became calm again. They had survived. The Sea Serpent was damaged, but still afloat. They had lost some of their supplies, but they were alive.

They saw land in the distance, a faint line on the horizon. They had made it. They had crossed the sea. They were one step closer to finding the Child of the Crimson Pendant.

Jax, his face grim, looked at the horizon. "That was no ordinary storm," he said. "You have powerful enemies. You will need more than just a ship and a map to survive what's coming."
