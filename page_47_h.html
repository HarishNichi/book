They climbed the scaffolding, their movements swift and silent. Kaelen went first, his strength and agility making the climb look easy. He helped Elara and Elias, pulling them up the rickety structure. They were almost at the top when a guard spotted them.

"There they are!" the guard shouted, raising the alarm. "Don't let them escape!"

Arrows began to fly, whizzing past them in the darkness. They had to move faster. They were exposed, and they were running out of time.
