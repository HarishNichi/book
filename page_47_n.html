Finally, Elara found it. Tucked away in a dusty corner, she found a small, leather-bound journal. It was the personal diary of the Alchemist King's wife, a powerful sorceress in her own right. The journal spoke of her love for her husband, her fears for their unborn child, and the growing threat of their enemies.

In the final entry, written in a shaky hand, she described a premonition, a vision of her husband's betrayal and death. She wrote of a desperate plan to save their child, to hide him away with a loyal servant, and to give him the Crimson Pendant, a key to his past and his future.

The journal ended with a heartbreaking plea: "Find him. Protect him. Tell him that his mother loved him."
