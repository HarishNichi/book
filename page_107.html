Act 2: Rise of the Storm

Scene 7: Rebirth of Will

Anirudh stood before the entrance to the ancient mountain cave, the same one where his training under Arjun had begun. The air here felt different now, charged with a quiet energy. The defeat in the tournament had been a brutal lesson, but the terror in the eyes of his villagers, facing the corrupted beasts, had been a more profound one.

He carried the incomplete scroll titled "To Rise, First Die." Before, it had been a riddle, a technique promising resurrection through a kind of symbolic death, but lacking crucial steps. Now, the chaos he'd faced, the raw survival instincts he'd tapped into while defending his home, had unlocked something within him.

He remembered the fluid evasion learned from dodging the Flame Serpent's fire, the precise movements needed to counter the Ice Tiger's sudden attacks, and the ability to sense unseen threats like the Phantom Crow.
