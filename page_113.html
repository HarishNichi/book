Act 2: Rise of the Storm

Scene: Rebirth of Will

Anirudh, back in the silent embrace of the mountain cave, felt a profound shift. The echoes of the village's cries still lingered, but they no longer ignited blind fury. Instead, they resonated with a calm determination.

He held the incomplete scroll, its title "To Rise, First Die" now shimmering with a new understanding. It wasn't about physical death, but the death of his past limitations, the shedding of his father's shadow.
